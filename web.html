Space Exploration

Space exploration is the use of astronomy and space technology to explore outer space. [1] While observation of objects in space predates reliable history, it was the development of large liquid-fueled rockets during the early 20th century that allowed space exploration to become a practical possibility.

🚀 Apollo 11 mission — First Moon landing, 1969

The twentieth century saw humanity break the bounds of Earth, with Sputnik 1 becoming the first artificial satellite. The Apollo program (1961–1972) landed humans on the Moon and revolutionized our perspective. [2] Today, rovers on Mars and probes beyond our solar system continue to expand knowledge.
